# Customer Interview Template — Reference

## Setup (5 min)
- "Tell me about your most recent project that used the platform."
- Capture: project name, model type, team size, current state in pipeline

## Pain (15 min)
- "What's the most frustrating thing about the platform right now?"
- "Walk me through your last bad day with the platform."
- Probe: what would have made it better? What would you have done differently?

## Workflow (10 min)
- "Walk me through how you submit a new training job."
- Watch them do it live; note every place they pause / re-read docs / ask for help

## Wishlist (5 min)
- "If you could ask for one platform feature next quarter, what?"
- "What about us would make you recommend the platform to a peer?"

## Synthesis (after 3 interviews)
- Cluster pains into 3-5 themes
- Distinguish feature gaps (need to build) from knowledge gaps (need to document)
- Rank by effort × impact
